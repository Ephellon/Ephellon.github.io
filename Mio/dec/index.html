Mi/o | Decompression

Mi/o is an optimized LZW algorithm, that also allows the use of the Base64 algorithms (atob and btoa in JavaScript). The Mio.dec method is used to decompress data. It automatically detects LZW or Base64 formatted strings.

When to use compression

Although Mi/o is fast, and lossless, I wouldn't trust the algorithms fully on sensitive data. Places you could use Mi/o:

  • Case studies
  • JavaScript's localStorage
  • Small-data applications (such as document processing)
